Alumni

You're Invited.

The Society of Software Engineers at RIT would like to cordially invite you to our annual Winter Ball event. Our event will be held in the Atrium of the Thomas Golisano College of Computing and Information Sciences (bldg. 70).

This year's theme is inspired by a Yacht Club event. Come enjoy a formal evening on the sea, under the stars, bubbly in hand, and in good company of your fellow SSE mates and other RIT Golisano alumni, staff, faculty, and current students. All aboard the SS-Yacht!
